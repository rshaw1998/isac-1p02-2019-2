Camelot Golf and Country Club

Readers Rankings - Canada Top 100 2018 #36

Scoregolf - Top 100 2018 #74

Camelot Golf and Country Club is one of the most beautiful 18 hole 72 par courses in the Eastern-Ontario region. The course feature two very different nines. The front nine has a more links style feel as opposed to the back nine which features alot of foerest and elevation changes. With the beautiful location on the hill top near the Ottawa River, this course has some of the most spectacular views in the area.

Scoregolf - Top 100 2018 #51

Golf Magazine - Top 100 2015 #55

Readers Rankings - Canada Top 100 2018 #54

The Heathlands course at TPC Toronto - Osprey Valley is one of three 18 hole course on the property on the outscruts of Toronto, in Caledon ON. The newest member of the TPC family and first in Canada with the recognition. THe Heathlands course is a links style course that has hoseted several junior tournaments including the North Amercican Cup between the CJGA Team Canada and IGT Team USA.

Feauture Architect

Thomas Mcbroom

In the past 25 years, McBroom has created a stunning collection of modern classics distinguished by their detail and craftsmanship, strategic intrigue and great beauty.Mr. McBroom's portfolio of courses include some of the most premier Private Clubs such as Wildfire, Rattlesnake Point, Lake Joseph and Heron Point and of course Camelot Golf & Country Club but to name a few. Most recently his notable designs include Tobiano (BC) and Links of Brunello (NS). Every Tom McBroom design captures the unique character of the landscape so that each course possesses its own distinct personality, charm and playing experience. Whether he's crafting a new course or restoring a weary masterpiece, Tom McBroom's passion to create courses that stir the golfer's soul is demonstrated proudly in every design that bears his signature(Camelot, n.d.).
